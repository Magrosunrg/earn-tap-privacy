# Earn-Tap Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: November 2025**

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Earn-Tap ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") collects, uses, and processes information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

By downloading and using Earn-Tap, you acknowledge that you have read and agree to this Privacy Policy.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide Directly
- **Optional User Data**: If you choose to use our leaderboard or social features, we may collect:
  - Display name or username
  - Profile information
  - Gameplay statistics

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically
- **Game Progress Data**: Tapping statistics, generator purchases, upgrades, achievements, companion status, quest progress, prestige level, statistics
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system version, app version
- **Usage Analytics**: Features accessed, time spent in-app, gameplay patterns
- **Crash Reports**: Error logs, performance data
- **Local Storage**: Game saves stored on your device via SharedPreferences (offline)

### 2.3 Third-Party Data Collection
- **Google Mobile Ads**: Limited data for ad targeting (aggregated, non-personally identifiable)
- **Google Play / Apple App Store**: Purchase history and payment information (handled directly by platform)
- **Analytics Services**: Crash reporting and usage patterns (Firebase or similar)

## 3. What We Do NOT Collect

Earn-Tap **does not require**:
- Email address or account creation
- Personal identification information
- Location data
- Contacts or social media credentials
- Payment information (handled by app stores)
- Camera or microphone access

## 4. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for:
- **Game Functionality**: Saving game progress, enabling features
- **Leaderboards & Social**: Displaying your ranking and guild progress (local only)
- **Analytics & Improvement**: Understanding gameplay patterns, fixing bugs, optimizing performance
- **Advertising**: Displaying relevant ads (optional; based on non-personal aggregated data)
- **Safety**: Preventing cheating, detecting crashes

## 5. Data Storage & Persistence

### 5.1 Local Storage
- Game progress is stored **locally on your device** using SharedPreferences
- Data is synced with iCloud (iOS) or Google Drive (Android) if you enable cloud backup
- No data is sent to our servers (offline-first design)

### 5.2 Cloud Backup (Optional)
- If you enable cloud sync, game data may be backed up to:
  - iCloud (iOS devices)
  - Google Drive (Android devices)
- These backups are encrypted and subject to Apple/Google's privacy policies

### 5.3 No Central Server
- Earn-Tap does **not operate a central server** to collect or store your data
- Leaderboards and social features are simulated locally on your device
- All multiplayer/social features use local simulation (no network transmission)

## 6. Third-Party Services

### 6.1 In-App Purchases
- Handled by Google Play Store or Apple App Store
- Payment information is **not** processed by us
- Subject to [Google Play Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) or [Apple Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/privacy/)

### 6.2 Advertising
- Google Mobile Ads SDK may collect:
  - Device identifiers
  - Aggregated usage data
  - Ad interaction data
- View [Google Ads Privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- **Disable personalized ads** in your device settings if desired

### 6.3 Analytics & Crash Reporting
- We may use Firebase Crashlytics or similar services to:
  - Track app crashes
  - Monitor performance
  - Improve stability
- Aggregated, non-personally identifiable data only
- View [Firebase Privacy](https://firebase.google.com/terms/analytics)

## 7. Data Security

We implement security measures including:
- Encrypted local storage on your device
- Secure app code (reviewed for vulnerabilities)
- No transmission of sensitive gameplay data
- Regular app updates for security patches

## 8. Your Privacy Rights

- **Access Your Data**: All game data is stored locally on your device; you can export it
- **Delete Your Data**: Uninstall the app to delete all local game data
- **Opt-Out of Ads**: Disable personalized ads in your device settings
- **Opt-Out of Analytics**: Contact us to disable analytics collection

## 9. GDPR, CCPA & Regional Compliance

### 9.1 GDPR (EU Users)
We process minimal personal data and base it on:
- Legitimate interest (improving the app)
- User consent (for analytics/ads)

You have rights to access, correct, or delete your data. Contact: privacy@earntap.com

### 9.2 CCPA (California Users)
We do **not** sell personal information. California residents have rights to:
- Know what data is collected
- Delete personal data
- Opt-out of targeted advertising

## 10. Children's Privacy

Earn-Tap is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If a parent/guardian believes their child provided personal information, contact us at: privacy@earntap.com

## 11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. Changes take effect when posted. Your continued use of Earn-Tap constitutes acceptance of changes.
